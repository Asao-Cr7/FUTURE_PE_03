### **Pro Tip: Authenticating KSIC Mysore Silk**

Authentic **KSIC Mysore Silk sarees** contain a **gold-silver zari seal** issued by the Karnataka Silk Industries Corporation.

When purchasing silk garments:

* Look for the official **KSIC hologram tag**
* Verify the **gold-silver zari mark** on the label
* Purchase from authorized retailers

This verification protects buyers from counterfeit silk products.
